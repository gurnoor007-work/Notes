# Exam Strategy
---
### Order to attempt
#### Round 1
1. Organic
2. Zoology
3. Botany
4. Inorganic
5. Physics
   - First attempt questions you're 100% sure about
   - Read the question, if it doesn't hit for 10 seconds, leave
   - if hits, check if it would take time or not
   - if it will take time, star it and go to next
   - if it won't take time, destroy it

#### Round 2
1. Verify Organic Questions which you starred
2. Verify Zoology questions which you starred
3. Verify Botany Questions which you starrred
4. Verify IOC Questions which you starred
5. Verify Physics questions which you starred

`At this point, you'll have enough questions done to gain a shit ton of confidence with plenty of time remaining`
<br/>
`I'm Assuming that Chemistry (_except Physical_), Biology are **Done**`

#### Round 3
1. Come back to Physics and do the lengthy ones
2. Complete Physical (Take your time, do it cleanly with no mistakes)

#### Round 4 (Even if 5 minutes remaining)
1. Do the questions of Physics which you thought you couldn't do (Try your best to think something)
